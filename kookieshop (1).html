About Kookies

Kookies is where elegance meets indulgence a sweet escape curated for the modern sweet tooth. We craft handcrafted cookies and drinks using only premium ingredients, with flavors that are as Instagram-worthy as they are irresistible.

But behind every batch is a personal story. It all started in a cozy kitchen in her Apartment, where Eli baked her very first cookies on a quiet, rainy afternoon. What began as a simple craving turned into a passion project and eventually, into Kookies. Every treat you taste carries the warmth of home, a sprinkle of love, and the joy of sharing something truly special.

From timeless classics to bold new bites, Kookies is here to bring sweetness, style, and soul to your every snack.
